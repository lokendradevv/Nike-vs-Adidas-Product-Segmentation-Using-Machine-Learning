# 👟 Nike vs Adidas: Product Segmentation & Market Analysis using Machine Learning

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **product segmentation analysis** on footwear products from two global sportswear giants — **Nike** and **Adidas**.  

Using **Machine Learning (K-Means Clustering)**, the project groups products based on pricing, discounts, ratings, and customer engagement to uncover brand strategies and market positioning patterns.

The analysis helps identify:
- Premium vs budget product categories
- Discount-driven strategies
- Customer engagement patterns
- Brand-wise product diversification

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze footwear product data of Nike and Adidas
- Perform customer/product segmentation using Machine Learning
- Compare pricing and discount strategies
- Discover premium and budget-oriented product groups
- Generate business insights from clustered data

# 📈 Key Findings

## Adidas Strategy
- Adidas products are distributed across multiple clusters.
- The brand follows a diversified product strategy.
- Heavy use of discounts and promotional pricing.
- Covers wider market segments from budget to premium.

## Nike Strategy
- Nike products are concentrated in fewer clusters.
- Strong focus on premium positioning.
- Limited discount strategy.
- More selective and brand-focused product placement.

---

# 🔍 Cluster Analysis

## Cluster 0 — Mid-Range Low Engagement Products
- Mid-priced products
- Low ratings and customer interaction
- Needs repositioning and better visibility

## Cluster 1 — Budget Friendly Products
- Affordable products
- Stable ratings
- Strong value-for-money segment

## Cluster 2 — High Discount Products
- Highest discounts
- Highest review counts
- Ideal for promotional campaigns
- Dominated mainly by Adidas

## Cluster 3 — Premium Products
- High-priced premium footwear
- Strong ratings and brand perception
- Dominated mainly by Nike

---

# 📌 Major Observations

- Adidas gives significantly more discounts compared to Nike.
- Nike focuses more on premium branding.
- Adidas captures broader customer segments.
- Nearly one-third of Nike products fall into premium categories.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### For Mid-Range Products
- Improve product quality and marketing visibility.

### For Budget Products
- Maintain competitive pricing and value-based promotions.

### For Discount Products
- Use seasonal campaigns and sales promotions.

### For Premium Products
- Strengthen luxury branding and flagship marketing strategies.
